EARTH

Earth is the third closest planet to the Sun, the densest and the fifth largest of the eight planets in the Solar System. The planet formed 4.56 billion years ago, and life emerged on its surface a billion years later. Since then, the Earth's biosphere has significantly altered the planet's atmosphere and other abiotic factors, allowing the proliferation of aerobic organisms, as well as the formation of an ozone layer, which, together with the Earth's magnetic field.
