Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System, both in diameter and in mass, and is the fifth closest to the Sun. Jupiter is composed mainly of hydrogen, with a quarter of its mass being composed of helium, although helium is only one tenth of the total number of molecules. The planet may also have a rocky core composed of heavier elements, although, like the other giant planets, it does not have a well-defined solid surface.
